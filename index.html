How to Use This Tool

Drag items from the left column into the right column to rank them in order of priority — #1 being highest priority. You can reorder items in the ranked list by dragging them up or down. Ranked items show your priority position. When finished, click "View My Rankings" to review and copy your results, or "Send Via Email" to email your results directly to Monique Barnhart-Tibero. To print and save your results for your records, select "Print/Save As A PDF".
